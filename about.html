     Tyler J Stannard is a current student at San Jose State University on the path to finish up his BFA in Digital Media Arts. His creative process is fueled with a strong passion for fantasy and the digital world, that interest has helped break his mindset from the boundaries of the physical realm of reality. In the arts Tyler is skilled in handful mediums and artist ideologies as he has studied art for several years across different schools, from Mission College to San Jose State University, along with acquiring the techniques of those art forms. He has done works in physical 2D realm of art and digital but has expanded into 3D forms and sculpture. His works prove to showcase an interactive and immersive experience to anyone whom experiences them.
